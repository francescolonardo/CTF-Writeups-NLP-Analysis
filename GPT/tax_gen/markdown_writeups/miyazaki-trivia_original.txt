<h2>Solution</h2>

<p>The website prints a message.</p>

<p><code>html
&lt;doctype html&gt;
&lt;html&gt;
    &lt;title&gt;AUCTF&lt;/title&gt;
    &lt;body&gt;
        &lt;h1&gt;Find this special file.&lt;/h1&gt;
    &lt;/body&gt;
&lt;/html&gt;
</code></p>

<p>Connecting to <code>http://challenges.auctf.com:30020/robots.txt</code> will give the following message.</p>

<p>```
VIDEO GAME TRIVIA: What is the adage of Byrgenwerth scholars?</p>

<p>MAKE a GET request to this page with a header named 'answer' to submit your answer.
```</p>

<p>The answer to the trivia is: <code>Fear the Old Blood</code>. So you can perform the requested HTTP GET and you will get the flag.</p>
