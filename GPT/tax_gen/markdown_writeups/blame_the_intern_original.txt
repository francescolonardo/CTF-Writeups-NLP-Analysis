<h1>Magpie2022 - Blame the Intern</h1>

<h3>Category: Web</h3>

<h2>### Author: Josh Novak (rm -rf /)</h2>

<h2>Description</h2>

<p>"It was the intern" said Jimmy. His boss was not happy. A vulnerable web application was hosted on company servers. See if you can find the flag using the interns app.</p>

<hr />

<h2>Hints</h2>

<ol>
<li>Luckily, the intern was vaccinated. He got his INJECTION recently.</li>
</ol>

<hr />

<h2>Solution</h2>

<ol>
<li>Download app.py and examine the render function</li>
<li>Determine that template injection is possible when passing things in the textbox</li>
<li>Input <code>{{ state.flag }}</code> (you could also do <code>{{ state }}</code> and get the flag)</li>
</ol>
