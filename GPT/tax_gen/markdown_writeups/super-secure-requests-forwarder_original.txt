<h1>Super Secure Requests Forwarder - Solution</h1>

<p><strong>Author</strong>: zeyu2001</p>

<p><strong>Category</strong>: Web</p>

<p>The validation mechanism is flawed - the URL is fetched once to check whether an SSRF is attempted, and then fetched a second time once it is determined to be legitimate.</p>

<p>We can run a server that gives two different responses, passing the check the first time and redirecting to <code>http://localhost/flag</code> the second time.</p>
