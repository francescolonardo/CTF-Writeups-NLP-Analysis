<h3>Solution</h3>

<p>At this point, you can spot two interesting cookies:
* <code>JWT 'identity'=31337</code>;
* <code>JWT 'secret'="dawgCTF?heckin#bamboozle"</code>.</p>

<p>Analyzing the capture, you can find two packets, #261 and #263, regarding a JWT-related endpoint.</p>
