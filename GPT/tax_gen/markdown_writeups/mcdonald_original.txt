<h2>Challenge</h2>

<blockquote>
  <p>Our web admin name's "Mc Donald" and he likes apples and always forgets to throw away his apple cores..</p>
  
  <p>http://35.207.91.38</p>
</blockquote>

<h2>Solution</h2>

<p>Visiting <code>http://35.207.91.38/robots.txt</code> will reveal the following content.</p>

<p><code>
User-agent: *
Disallow: /backup/.DS_Store
</code></p>

<p>So the <code>http://35.207.91.38/backup/.DS_Store</code> will download a <code>.DS_Store</code> file.</p>

<p>According to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/.DS_Store">Wikipedia</a>:</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>In the Apple macOS operating system, .DS_Store is a file that stores custom attributes of its containing folder, such as the position of icons or the choice of a background image.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>The <a href="https://github.com/lijiejie/ds_store_exp">https://github.com/lijiejie/ds<em>store</em>exp</a> script could be used to download and extract hidden data.</p>

<p><code>
./ds_store_exp.py http://35.207.91.38/backup/.DS_Store
</code></p>

<p>The flag will be into <code>backup/b/a/c/flag.txt</code>.</p>
