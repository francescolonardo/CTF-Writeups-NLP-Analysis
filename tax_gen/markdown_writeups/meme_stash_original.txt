<h2>Solution</h2>

<p>A basic enumeration will reveal the presence of a Git repository.</p>

<p>```
root@m3ss4p0:~# dirb http://meme-stash.darkarmy.xyz/ /usr/share/dirb/wordlists/common.txt -z 200</p>

<p>http://meme-stash.darkarmy.xyz/.git/HEAD
```</p>
