<h2>description: Filtered SQL injection</h2>

<h1>no pass needed</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>

<p>It's all about who you know and I know 'admin'.</p>

<p>http://web.chal.csaw.io:5001</p>

<h2>Solution</h2>

<ul>
<li>The username is reflected back into the username field (its <code>value</code> attribute) after failed authentication</li>
<li>By fuzzing username inputs, we can find that anything after a whitespace is removed.</li>
<li>Furthermore, 'admin' is replaced <strong>non-recursively</strong>.</li>
</ul>

<p>We can exploit an SQL injection in the username parameter. The payload is:</p>

<p><code>text
username=adadminmin';#&amp;password=
</code></p>

<p>This will translate to the query:</p>

<p><code>text
SELECT * FROM users WHERE username='admin';#
</code></p>

<p>The flag is <code>flag{wh0_n3ed5_a_p4ssw0rd_anyw4y}</code>.</p>
