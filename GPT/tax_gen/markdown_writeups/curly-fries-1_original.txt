<h1>Curly Fries 1 - Web</h1>

<p>Author - <a href="https://github.com/UnknownAbyss">UnknownAbyss</a></p>

<p>Requirements : cURL</p>

<hr />

<p>The challenge gives us the following text -</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>Normal fries are nice, but everything's better with a curl in it. The flag is right in front of you.</p>
</blockquote>

<p><code>
very.uniquename.xyz:8880
</code></p>

<h4>Upon opening the link we see -</h4>

<p align="center"><img src="https://i.imgur.com/pa1MPge.png" alt="Chall Image"></p>

<p>This screams sweden. So we try to set the language headers to swedish</p>

<p><code>sh
curl -H "Accept-Language: sv-SE" very.uniquename.xyz:8880
</code></p>

<p>This gives us the flag...
<code>
dsc{1_l0v3_sw3d3n}
</code></p>
