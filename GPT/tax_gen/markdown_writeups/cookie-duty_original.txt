<h2>Solution</h2>

<p>After submitting the form you can see a cookie:</p>

<p><code>
not_admin : 1
</code></p>

<p>It's sufficient to change that cookie to the value 0 and to refresh the page in order to retrieve the flag:</p>

<p><code>
TUCTF{D0nt_Sk1p_C00k13_Duty}
</code></p>
